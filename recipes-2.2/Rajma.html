Rajma Chawal

Rajma Chawal Supremacy

Every cuisine has its own unique signature, in terms of its spices, ingredients and cooking techniques. This Kashmiri style Rajma is not your usual Rajma

Ingredients

  • half cup rajma
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • half tsp cumin seeds
  • a quarter tsp asafoetida
  • half cup sliced onions
  • half tsp ginger garlic paste
  • half cup chopped tomatoes
  • a quarter tsp turmeric powder
  • half tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander-cumin seeds powder
  • 1 tvsp finely chopped coriander

Steps

  1. To make Rajma Chawal, heat the oil in a pressure cooker and add he cumin seeds and asafoetuda and saute on a mediu, flame for a few seconds
  2. add the onions and ginger garlic paste and saute on a medium flame for 2 to 3 minutes
  3. add the tomatoes, tumeric powder, chilli powder, coriander-cumin seeds powder and salt, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 1 to 2 minutes while stiring occasionally
  4. Add the rajma and 1 cup of water mix well and pressure cooker for 4 whistles
  5. allow the steam to escape before opening the lid
  6. add the cooked rice and mix gently and cook on a medium flame for 2 minutes while stirring occasionally
  7. add th coriander and mix well
